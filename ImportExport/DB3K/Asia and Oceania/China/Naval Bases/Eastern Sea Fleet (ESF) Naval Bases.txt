Version: 1.0 (19/03/23)


- Ningbo ESF Headquarters (Chinese: 寧波艦隊司令部) Large PLAN building.
- Shanghai Naval Base (Chinese: 上海海軍基地) Located on the southern estuary of the Yangtze River, with the Huangpu River flowing through it. 
- Xiangshan Naval Base (Chinese: 象山海軍基地) ESF submarine base that also has a tunnel, Structure (Underground Pier [Very Large] Building Underground
- Zhoushan Naval Base (Chinese: 舟山海軍基地) Urbanised archipelago with the administrative status of a prefecture-level city in the eastern Chinese province of Zhejiang. 
- Fujian Naval Base (Chinese: 福建海軍基地)  Province on the southeastern coast of China. Fujian is bordered by Zhejiang to the north, Jiangxi to the west, Guangdong to the south, and the Taiwan Strait to the east. Its capital is Fuzhou.
